In this guide, we will be installing the [StarBar Star Power Button Upgrade](https://shop.retrocultmods.com/products/starbar-star-power-button-upgrade-for-crkd-lp-guitar-controllers) mod. The StarBar can be used on all current models of CRKD Guitar Controller.

# Required Tools

- T10 Screwdriver/Bit
- PH1 Screwdriver/Bit

[If you do not have these screwdriver/bits already, we suggest this screwdriver kit.](https://www.amazon.com/gp/product/B0CCTNX9GY?ie=UTF8&th=1&linkCode=sl1&tag=retrocultmods-20&linkId=60d41473b70d0554fe64324077ad9a00&language=en_US&ref_=as_li_ss_tl)
